Best Practices

  • Use software from trusted sources only
  • Don't visit suspicious websites
  • Don't execute attachments in emails or messaging apps unless you are sure of what it does
  • Keep your anti-virus software up to date
  • Make sure you have the latest security updates for your operating system and programs

What is a Trojan Horse?

A Trojan horse is a non-replicating program that performs malicious activities under the guise of being legitimate. They can be used to destroy data and extract user information.

What should I watch out for?

  • Suspicious activity regarding deletion or modification of files on your system
  • Any services requiring credentials being accessed without your knowledge.
