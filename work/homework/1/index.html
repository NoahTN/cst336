MALICIOUS CODE

What is Malicious Code?

Malicious code is a broad category of programs that cause adverse effects to computers and networks.

Then, what is a Computer Virus?

A computer virus is a program that can self replicate and attach itself to other files and processes. Although a computer virus is the most common form of malicious code, the term is often misused to refer to other types.
