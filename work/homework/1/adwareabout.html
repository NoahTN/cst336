Best Practices

  • Use software from trusted sources only

  • Don't visit suspicious websites

  • Don't execute attachments in emails or messaging apps unless you are sure of what it does

  • Keep your anti-virus software up to date

  • Make sure you have the latest security updates for your operating system and programs

What is Adware?

Adware is a program that displays advertising for a product on top of other programs.

What should I watch out for?

  • Your web browser's homepage being changed unwillingly

  • Toolbars and other software being installed without your permission

  • Pop-up windows with advertisement even when not web browsing
